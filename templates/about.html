INUA COLLECTIVE SOCIETY

is a non-profit organization with operations in the BC Lower mainland. INUA is a Swahili word which translates to elevate or uplift. INUA Collective society strives to elevate and up-skill the welfare of BIPOC and other racialized communities in BC lower mainland. INUA Collective Society hopes to achieve its goal through supporting, showcasing, and empowering the communities they work with through Arts, Sports, capacity building and entrepreneurship.
